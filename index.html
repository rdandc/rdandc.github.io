About Us

Based in San Diego, California, Ramos D&C is a new Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB) specializing in traffic electrical design. We draw on decades of diverse project experience to deliver on a wide range of jobs.

Our Areas of Expertise

Traffic Signaling

Traffic Signal design for both intersections and ramp metering.

Traffic Analysis

Utilizing Trafficware's Synchro Studio® and Sim Traffic® to provide the most accurate Traffic estimates for any roadway.

Lighting

Deploy lighting on Surface Streets, Bike Paths, Highways, Intersections, Roundabouts, Bypass, In-pavement Lighting System, & Pedestrian Lighting.

EV Charging Infrastructure

Prepare your project for the electrification of transportation with infrastructe to support a wide variety of commercial vehicle charging stations.

Detection Systems

Deploy Inductive Hardwire, Radar, Video Detection, or new Intelligent Transportation Systems with Wifi proximity detection.

Fiber Optics

Make your jobsite a data hub with fast fiber connections to all your systems.

Plans, Specifications, and Construction Estimates (PS&E)

End to end job analysis.

Construction

We can deliver Contract Change Order design plans & estimates for any job.
